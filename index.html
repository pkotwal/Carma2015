CarMa, MS TSEC's flagship inter collegiate festival, is a one of its kind venture that focuses on honing the management skills of students with a plethora of management related events and games and a host of fun events as well. It is the first inter collegiate festival of the academic year. As the name suggests (CAReer MAnagement), the events in this festival tend to hone management skills in one way or the other. Over the years, CARMA has opened up new vistas to explore in post-graduate study and has helped students realize their entrepreneurial potential, if not improvise their soft skills over the numerous events it hosts. CarMa witnesses participation from all across the country for the 20 plus events it hosts. The tradition of conducting the cities best literary events that include JAM, Turncoats, MUNs, Debates and Quizzes that initiated in 2003 continues up to this very year. The Model United Nations Conference that occurs during CarMa in TSEC is one of the most anticipated MUNs across the national debating circuit. Every event being a gem of its own, CarMa is a world in itself.
